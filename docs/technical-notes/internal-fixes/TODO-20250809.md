## Product TODO — Small Business Financing & Growth Roadmap

This document outlines high‑impact features to help small businesses manage cash flow, get paid faster, and scale. It’s tailored to the current stack and code structure.

### Current capabilities (from code review)
- Invoices, items, discounts, history, PDF export, attachments: `api/routers/invoices.py`, `api/utils/pdf_generator.py`, `ui/src/components/invoices/*`
- Payments (manual entry), dashboard stats: `api/routers/payments.py`, `ui/src/pages/Payments.tsx`, `ui/src/lib/api.ts`
- Multi‑currency with rates service: `api/routers/currency.py`, `api/services/currency_service.py`
- Email sending (providers), invoice email with PDF: `api/services/email_service.py`, `api/routers/email.py`
- Notifications settings (email), plumbing in place: `api/models/models_per_tenant.py` (EmailNotificationSettings), `api/services/notification_service.py`
- AI/MCP assistant, PDF invoice import: `api/routers/ai.py`, `api/MCP/*`, `api/routers/pdf_processor.py`, `api/docs/PDF_IMPORT_README.md`
- i18n (web+mobile), Slack integration, analytics hooks: `ui/src/i18n/*`, `api/routers/slack_simplified.py`, `api/services/analytics_service.py`
- Multi‑tenant + super admin: `api/routers/super_admin.py`, `api/routers/auth.py`

Gaps vs. typical SMB needs: online payments, customer portal, quotes → invoices, automated reminders/dunning, expenses and receipt OCR, bank feeds + reconciliation, richer reports (AR aging, P&L, cash flow, sales tax), subscriptions/recurring billing, PO/bills, item catalog/inventory‑light, financing readiness.

---

### Tier 0 — Quick wins (1–2 weeks)
1) Automated invoice reminders (email; later SMS/WhatsApp)
   - Scope: schedule reminders at issue, 3/7 days before due, on due, 3/7/14 days overdue; per‑tenant templates.
   - Backend: add scheduler (APScheduler) or lightweight async task runner; new endpoints to opt‑in per invoice/client/tenant; use `EmailService` and `EmailNotificationSettings`.
   - UI: toggles in `Settings → Notifications`; per‑invoice reminder checkbox in `InvoiceForm`.
   - Success: time‑to‑pay ↓, overdue rate ↓, reminder send rate.

2) Quotes/Estimates → Convert to Invoice
   - Scope: new `Quote` entity; convert to invoice with preserved items/discounts.
   - Backend: `models_per_tenant.Quote`, CRUD router `api/routers/quotes.py`.
   - UI: `ui/src/pages/Quotes.tsx`, reuse `InvoiceForm` read‑only mode.
   - Success: quote acceptance rate, conversion time.

3) Item catalog (Products/Services)
   - Scope: reusable items with price, tax code, SKU, default description.
   - Backend: `Product` model, CRUD router.
   - UI: selector in `InvoiceForm` with autocomplete.
   - Success: time to create invoice ↓, line‑item errors ↓.

4) Basic tax handling
   - Scope: per‑item and invoice‑level tax rates; tax summary on PDF; tax report export.
   - Backend: `TaxRate`, apply in totals; `/reports/tax`.
   - UI: tax fields on items; settings to define rates.

---

### Tier 1 — Get paid faster (4–6 weeks)
5) Online payments (Stripe first; PayPal next)
   - Scope: payment links on invoices, card + ACH, partial payments, webhooks, fees captured.
   - Backend: `payments/providers/stripe.py`, checkout sessions, webhooks; map to `Payment` records.
   - UI: “Pay now” button, status chips, success/failure flows.
   - Success: online collection rate, DSO ↓, failed payment rate.

6) Customer portal
   - Scope: secure portal for clients to view/pay invoices, download PDFs, see statement.
   - Backend: short‑lived signed links/JWT; client‑scoped endpoints.
   - UI: minimal portal page/theme.

7) Recurring billing & subscriptions
   - Scope: leverage existing `Invoice.is_recurring`; add schedules, auto‑generation, stored payment methods, dunning retries.
   - Backend: scheduler + Stripe subscriptions or in‑house recurrence with tokenized payments.
   - UI: recurrence plan builder in `InvoiceForm`.

8) AR aging & collections dashboard
   - Scope: 0–30/31–60/61–90/>90 buckets, client statements, promise‑to‑pay tracking.
   - Backend: `/reports/ar-aging`, statements endpoint.
   - UI: AR aging page + export.

---

### Tier 2 — Cash flow control (6–10 weeks)
9) Expenses + receipt capture (OCR)
   - Scope: expenses, categories, vendor, tax; mobile receipt scan; attach to P&L and cash flow.
   - Backend: `Expense` model/router; OCR via Tesseract/LangChain; link to reports.
   - UI: Expenses pages; mobile capture.

10) Cash flow forecasting (30/60/90 days)
   - Scope: project inflows from invoices/subscriptions + outflows from expenses; scenarios.
   - Backend: forecast service using due dates, payment probabilities, seasonality.
   - UI: chart + scenarios.

11) Sales tax/VAT reports
   - Scope: per jurisdiction summaries; export to CSV.
   - Backend: `/reports/tax`; leverage Tier 0 tax fields.

12) Advanced reminders & dunning
   - Scope: multi‑step sequences, time‑of‑day windows, per‑customer rules, SMS (Twilio) and WhatsApp.
   - Backend: add Twilio provider; schedule queue; opt‑in/out at client level.

---

### Tier 3 — Scale & integrations (10–16 weeks)
13) Bank feeds + reconciliation (Plaid)
   - Scope: fetch transactions; auto‑match deposits to paid invoices; manual reconcile UI.
   - Backend: Plaid link/token exchange, transaction sync; reconciliation rules.
   - UI: bank feed list, matching suggestions.

14) Accounting integrations (QuickBooks/Xero)
   - Scope: push invoices/payments, import chart of accounts, export expenses.
   - Backend: connectors; background sync jobs; mapping tables.

15) Light inventory & COGS
   - Scope: product stock qty, cost, margin on invoice; low‑stock alerts.
   - Backend: extend `Product` and movement logs.

16) Purchase orders & vendor bills (AP)
   - Scope: PO → bill → payment; approvals; attach receipts.
   - Backend: PO/Bill models + routers; statuses and approval workflow.

17) Financing readiness & insights
   - Scope: revenue trends, gross margins, AR turnover, MRR (if subs), export lender packet; partner referral links.
   - Backend: analytics service, export generator (PDF/CSV), partner webhooks.

---
