## Conversion Triggers

### Strong Business Indicators
These actions strongly suggest business use and should trigger upgrade prompts:

1. **Attempting to create API key** → 80% business
2. **Attempting to add second user** → 95% business
3. **Creating recurring invoices** → 85% business
4. **Uploading multiple files** → 70% business
5. **Setting up webhooks** → 75% business
6. **Creating approval workflows** → 90% business
7. **Managing inventory** → 90% business

### Soft Business Indicators
These patterns suggest growing business use:

1. **Consistent high volume** (40+ invoices/month for 3 months)
2. **Professional invoice language** (LLC, Inc, Corp in invoices)
3. **Multiple clients** (15+ active clients)
4. **Regular usage pattern** (daily/weekly consistent use)
5. **Business email domain** (company.com vs gmail.com)
