# AI Services & Business Intelligence

Leverage state-of-the-art AI to automate your workflows and gain deeper insights into your business data.

## Overview

YourFinanceWORKS is built with AI at its core. From processing receipts to answering complex business questions, our AI services save you time and provide a competitive edge.

## 1. AI-Powered Automation

Let the machine handle the repetitive tasks.

- **Expense OCR**: Upload images or PDFs of receipts. Our AI extracts the vendor, amount, date, and tax with high accuracy, eliminating manual entry.
- **Email Ingestion (AI-Powered)**: Forward receipts or invoices to your dedicated email address. Our AI automatically classifies the email, extracts data from the body, and processes any attachments using OCR.
- **Smart Categorization**: The system automatically suggests the best accounting category for every expense based on historical patterns and industry standards.
- **Automatic Invoicing**: Draft invoices by simple uploading quotes or work orders; the AI identifies the line items for you.

## 2. The AI Assistant (MCP-Powered)

Ask your data anything in plain English.

- **Natural Language Queries**: Instead of digging through reports, just ask:
  - "Who has unpaid invoices over $1,000?"
  - "How much did I spend on travel last quarter?"
  - "Who are my top 5 customers by revenue?"
- **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**: Our assistant uses MCP to securely access your real business data, ensuring answers are accurate and up-to-date.
- **General Assistance**: Beyond your data, the assistant can help with general business advice, tax explanations, or drafting professional emails.

## 3. Configuration and Priority

Tailor the AI to your needs (Settings -> AI Configuration).

- **Provider Support**: Use OpenAI (GPT-4), Ollama (Local), or other supported providers.
- **Priority System**: Choose which AI model handles specifically tasks—use a faster model for OCR and a more powerful one for complex data analysis.
- **Prompt Management**: Customize the "personality" and system instructions of your AI assistant.

## 4. Privacy and Security

Your data stays yours.

- **Isolated Context**: The AI only sees the data for your specific organization.
- **Secure API Usage**: Communication with AI providers (like OpenAI) is encrypted and uses only the data necessary to answer your specific query.

---

### Pro Tips

- **Be Specific**: When asking the AI Assistant questions, include date ranges like "in 2024" or "last month" for better accuracy.
- **Check the OCR**: While our AI is highly accurate, always give a quick glance at the extracted data before saving to ensure 100% precision.
