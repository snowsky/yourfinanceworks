# Expense & Outbound Intelligence

Control costs and optimize your business expenditure with our intelligent expense tracking and analysis system.

## Overview

Managing expenses is about more than just logging receipts; it's about understanding where your money is going and ensuring every outbound dollar is justified and properly categorized.

## 1. Intelligent Data Entry

Stop wasting time on manual data entry.

- **AI-Powered OCR**: Upload a photo or PDF of your receipt, and our AI will automatically extract the date, vendor, amount, and tax.
- **Batch Processing**: Upload multiple receipts at once and let the background worker process them all.
- **Camera Upload**: If using the mobile-responsive web app, you can take photos of receipts directly into the system.

## 2. Categorization and Organization

Clean data leads to clear insights.

- **Smart Categories**: The AI suggests categories (Travel, Office Supplies, Meals, etc.) based on the vendor and description.
- **Custom Labels**: Add your own tags for project-specific tracking or internal accounting codes.
- **Attachment Storage**: Every expense is permanently linked to its receipt image for audit purposes.

## 3. Approval Workflows

Ensure compliance and control spending across your organization.

- **Multi-Level Approvals (Coming Soon)**: Planned support for hierarchies where expenses above a certain threshold require manager sign-off.
- **Status Tracking**: Monitor expenses as they move from "Draft" -> "Pending Approval" -> "Approved".
- **Audit Trail**: Every approval action is logged, showing exactly who approved what and when.

## 4. Analytics and Outbound Strategy

Turn expense data into business intelligence.

- **Spend Analysis**: View charts and breakdowns of your top spending categories.
- **Trend Detection**: Identify seasonality in your costs or unexpected spikes in specific areas.
- **Reporting**: Generate expense summaries for tax preparation or internal budget reviews.

---

### Pro Tips

- **Search by Receipt Text**: Our global search allows you to find expenses by searching for text _inside_ the attached receipt images.
- **Mobile Efficiency**: Log your expenses as they happen to avoid a backlog of receipts at the end of the month.
