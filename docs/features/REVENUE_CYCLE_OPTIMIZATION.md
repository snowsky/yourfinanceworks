# Revenue Cycle Optimization

This guide explores the end-to-end revenue cycle in YourFinanceWORKS, from initial document generation to final payment reconciliation.

## Overview

A streamlined revenue cycle is critical for business health. YourFinanceWORKS automates many of these steps to ensure you get paid faster and maintain professional relationships with your clients.

## 1. Client Management

Before you can invoice, you need a solid foundation of client data.

- **Profiles**: Store contact details, billing addresses, and tax information.
- **History**: View all past invoices and payment statuses for a specific client.
- **Notes**: Keep track of special billing arrangements or communication history.

## 2. Professional Invoicing

Our invoicing system is designed to be both powerful and easy to use.

- **Templates**: Choose from professional layouts or customize your own (Settings -> Invoice Templates).
- **AI-Powered Itemization**: The AI can help you categorize items or even draft invoice descriptions based on your provided data.
- **VAT/GST Support**: Configurable tax rates ensure compliance with local regulations.
- **Drafting**: Save invoices as drafts and preview the final PDF before sending.

## 3. Delivery and Automation

Getting the invoice to the client is just the beginning.

- **Email Delivery**: Send professional PDF invoices directly from the app (Requires email provider configuration).
- **Async Processing**: Large batch uploads or OCR-based invoice generation happen in the background, keeping the UI responsive.
- **Payment Reminders**: Set up automatic notifications for invoices approaching or past their due date.

## 4. Payment Tracking

Keep a real-time pulse on your accounts receivable.

- **Status Dashboard**: Instantly see which invoices are "Sent", "Overdue", or "Paid".
- **Partial Payments**: Record milestones or deposits against a single invoice.
- **Payment Methods**: Track how you were paid (Bank Transfer, Credit Card, Cash, etc.) for better reporting.

## 5. Reconciliation

The cycle completes when your bank statement matches your records.

- **One-Click Reconciliation**: Match incoming payments to outstanding invoices.
- **Automatic Status Updates**: Once a payment is matched, the invoice is automatically marked as "Paid".

---

### Pro Tips

- **Use OCR**: Instead of manual entry, upload previous project quotes or work orders to let the AI draft your invoice.
- **Monitor Overdue Invoices**: Use the "Who owes me money?" natural language query in the AI Assistant for a quick summary.
