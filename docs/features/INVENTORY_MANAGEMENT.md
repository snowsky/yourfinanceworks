# Inventory Management & Financial Integration

Track your stock levels, manage product costs, and seamlessly integrate inventory details into your invoicing workflow.

## Overview

The Inventory Management system in YourFinanceWORKS is designed to bridge the gap between your physical stock and your financial records. Every item you sell or use can be tracked for both quantity and cost.

## 1. Product & Service Catalog

Maintain a centralized database of everything you sell or buy.

- **Item Profiles**: Store SKUs, descriptions, default unit prices, and tax rates.
- **Cost Tracking**: Record the "Unit Cost" for items to accurately calculate your profit margins.
- **Categorization**: Group items by type (e.g., "Hardware", "Consulting", "Software Licenses") for better reporting.

## 2. Stock Level Monitoring

Never run out of what you need.

- **Stock Tracking**: Enable tracking for physical goods to keep a real-time count of available units.
- **Automatic Deductions**: When an invoice is marked as "Sent" or "Paid", the associated inventory items are automatically deducted from your stock.
- **Low Stock Alerts**: (Future Feature) Receive notifications when items fall below your defined minimum threshold.

## 3. Financial Integration

Inventory and Invoicing, working together.

- **One-Click Invoicing**: When creating a [new invoice](../features/REVENUE_CYCLE_OPTIMIZATION.md), simply select items from your inventory to auto-populate descriptions and prices.
- **Margin Analysis**: By comparing "Unit Cost" to "Unit Price" on your invoices, the system can provide deep insights into your most profitable products.
- **Asset Valuation**: Use the Inventory Dashboard to see the total current value of your on-hand stock for balance sheet reporting.

## 4. Maintenance and History

Track the lifecycle of your items.

- **Edit & Update**: Easily update prices or descriptions globally to ensure all new invoices use the most current data.
- **Transaction History**: View a history of every time an item was added to stock or sold via an invoice.

---

### Pro Tips

- **Use Bulk Actions**: Use the "Export" and "Import" features to manage hundreds of inventory items at once using spreadsheets.
- **Non-Physical Items**: You can use inventory for "Services" (like hourly consulting) by disabling stock tracking. This allows you to still use "One-Click Invoicing" and track service revenue trends.
