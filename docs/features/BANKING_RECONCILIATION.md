# Banking & Reconciliation

Ensure 100% accounting accuracy with our automated banking and reconciliation tools.

## Overview

Reconciliation is the process of ensuring that your internal financial records match your real-world bank statements. YourFinanceWORKS makes this typically tedious task efficient and accurate.

## 1. Bank Statement Processing

Import your data without manual typing.

- **Automated Extraction**: Upload your bank statement PDF, and our AI-powered processing system will extract transaction dates, descriptions, and amounts.
- **Format Support**: We support a wide range of bank statement formats, with regex and AI-based parsing to ensure high accuracy.
- **Data Security**: Your financial data is handled securely and isolated within your tenant-specific database.

## 2. Smart Transaction Matching

Connect your records to reality.

- **Auto-Matching**: The system automatically suggests matches between bank transactions and your logged invoices or expenses based on amounts and dates.
- **One-Click Approval**: Simply review and approve recommended matches to update your books instantly.
- **Manual Overrides**: For complex cases, you can manually bridge transactions to multiple invoices or record them as new expenses on the fly.

## 3. Financial Integrity

Maintain a "Source of Truth".

- **Real-Time Balances**: See your adjusted balance as you reconcile transactions.
- **Error Detection**: Quickly identify missing invoices or unexplained expenses that appear on your statement but not in your app.
- **Audit Readiness**: A fully reconciled system means you are always ready for tax season or financial audits.

---

### Pro Tips

- **Upload Regularly**: Upload your bank statements weekly to keep your financial dashboard as accurate as possible.
- **Use Clear Descriptions**: The better your invoice and expense descriptions, the more accurately our AI can auto-match them to your bank statements.
