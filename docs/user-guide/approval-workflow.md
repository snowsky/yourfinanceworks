# Expense Approval Workflow User Guide

## Overview

The expense approval workflow ensures that all expenses follow your organization's approval policies before reimbursement. This guide covers how to submit expenses for approval, track their status, and manage the approval process.

## For Employees: Submitting Expenses for Approval

### How to Submit an Expense for Approval

1. **Create Your Expense**
   - Navigate to "New Expense" from the main menu
   - Fill in all required fields (amount, date, category, description)
   - Upload receipts and supporting documentation
   - Save the expense as a draft

2. **Submit for Approval**
   - Click the "Submit for Approval" button on your expense
   - Review the expense details one final time
   - Confirm submission - once submitted, you cannot edit the expense
   - You'll receive a confirmation that your expense has been submitted

3. **Track Your Expense Status**
   - View your expenses list to see current approval status
   - Status indicators show: Pending Approval, Approved, Rejected, or Resubmitted
   - Click on any expense to see detailed approval history

### Understanding Expense Status

- **Draft**: Expense created but not yet submitted for approval
- **Pending Approval**: Submitted and waiting for manager review
- **Approved**: Approved by manager and ready for reimbursement
- **Rejected**: Rejected by manager with reason provided
- **Resubmitted**: Previously rejected expense that has been resubmitted

### What Happens After Submission

1. **Automatic Assignment**: The system automatically assigns your expense to the appropriate approver based on:
   - Expense amount
   - Expense category
   - Your organizational hierarchy
   - Configured approval rules

2. **Notifications**: Your approver receives an email notification about the pending expense

3. **Multi-Level Approval**: High-value expenses may require multiple approval levels

### If Your Expense is Rejected

1. **Review the Rejection Reason**: Check the detailed reason provided by your manager
2. **Make Necessary Changes**: Address the issues mentioned in the rejection
3. **Resubmit**: Click "Resubmit" to send the expense back for approval
4. **Contact Your Manager**: If you need clarification on the rejection reason

## For Managers: Reviewing and Approving Expenses

### Accessing Your Approval Dashboard

1. Navigate to the "Approvals" section from the main menu
2. Your dashboard shows:
   - Number of pending approvals
   - List of expenses awaiting your review
   - Filtering options by date, amount, or employee

### Reviewing an Expense

1. **Click on any pending expense** to open the detailed view
2. **Review all information**:
   - Expense amount and category
   - Date and description
   - Attached receipts and documentation
   - Employee information
   - Approval history (for multi-level approvals)

3. **Check for Policy Compliance**:
   - Verify expense amount is within policy limits
   - Ensure proper documentation is provided
   - Confirm expense category is appropriate
   - Review business justification

### Making Approval Decisions

#### To Approve an Expense:
1. Click the "Approve" button
2. Add optional notes for the employee
3. Confirm your approval
4. The employee receives automatic notification

#### To Reject an Expense:
1. Click the "Reject" button
2. **Required**: Provide a clear rejection reason
3. Explain what needs to be corrected
4. Confirm rejection
5. The employee receives notification with your feedback

### Managing Your Approval Workload

- **Use Filters**: Sort by amount, date, or employee to prioritize reviews
- **Bulk Actions**: Select multiple expenses for batch approval (when appropriate)
- **Set Reminders**: Configure notification preferences for pending approvals
- **Delegate Authority**: Set up temporary delegates when you're unavailable

### Approval Delegation

When you're out of office or unavailable:

1. Go to "Approval Settings" → "Delegation"
2. Select a delegate from your team
3. Set start and end dates for the delegation
4. Choose delegation scope (all expenses or specific criteria)
5. Your delegate receives notification of their temporary authority

## Understanding Approval Rules

### How Approval Rules Work

Your organization's approval rules determine:
- **Who** needs to approve different types of expenses
- **When** multiple approval levels are required
- **What** expense amounts trigger different approval workflows

### Common Approval Scenarios

1. **Small Expenses**: May be auto-approved or require single manager approval
2. **Medium Expenses**: Typically require direct manager approval
3. **Large Expenses**: May require multiple levels (manager → director → VP)
4. **Specific Categories**: Some expense types may have special approval requirements

### Multi-Level Approval Process

For high-value expenses:
1. **Level 1**: Direct manager reviews and approves
2. **Level 2**: Automatically routes to next level approver
3. **Level 3+**: Continues up the approval chain as configured
4. **Final Approval**: Expense is approved when all required levels complete

## Notifications and Reminders

### Email Notifications

You'll receive notifications for:
- **Employees**: Expense approved, rejected, or requires additional information
- **Managers**: New expense submitted, approval reminders, escalations

### In-App Notifications

- Dashboard badges show pending approval counts
- Real-time updates when expense status changes
- Reminder notifications for overdue approvals

### Notification Preferences

Customize your notification settings:
1. Go to "Settings" → "Notifications"
2. Choose email frequency (immediate, daily digest, weekly summary)
3. Select which events trigger notifications
4. Set reminder intervals for pending approvals

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "I can't submit my expense for approval"
- Ensure all required fields are completed
- Check that receipts are uploaded
- Verify expense amount is within policy limits
- Contact your manager if approval rules are unclear

### "My expense is stuck in pending status"
- Check who the current approver is
- Contact the approver directly if urgent
- Verify the approver hasn't delegated authority to someone else
- Escalate to HR or finance if approver is unavailable

### "I don't see expenses assigned to me for approval"
- Check your approval permissions with your administrator
- Verify you're looking in the correct date range
- Ensure expenses haven't been delegated to someone else
- Contact IT support if technical issues persist

### "I need to modify an expense after submission"
- Contact your approver to reject the expense
- Make necessary changes to the rejected expense
- Resubmit for approval
- For urgent changes, contact your manager directly

- Keep the approval process moving efficiently
