# Getting Started Guide

## Overview

Welcome to YourFinanceWORKS! This comprehensive guide will walk you through your first steps with our modern financial management system. Whether you're a freelancer, small business owner, or accountant, this guide will help you get up and running quickly.

### What is YourFinanceWORKS?

YourFinanceWORKS is a complete financial management platform that helps you:
- **Create professional invoices** with automatic numbering and PDF generation
- **Manage client relationships** with CRM capabilities and note-taking
- **Track expenses** and receipts with AI-powered processing
- **Generate reports** and analytics for business insights
- **Send invoices via email** with professional PDF attachments
- **Process bank statements** and receipts automatically
- **Manage approvals** for expense and invoice workflows

### Trial Period

New accounts can apply for a **30-day free trial** with full access to all features. This allows you to explore the platform and decide if it meets your needs before purchasing.

## Step 1: Account Creation

### Method 1: Sign Up for New Account

1. **Navigate to the Application**
   - Open your web browser
   - Go to your organization's YourFinanceWORKS URL (provided by your administrator)
   - Or visit the demo site: `https://demo.yourfinanceworks.com`

2. **Click Sign Up**
   - On the login page, click the **"Sign Up"** button
   - Or click **"Create Account"** if available

3. **Enter Your Information**
   ```
   Full Name: [Your full name]
   Email: [your-email@domain.com]
   Password: [Create a strong password - minimum 8 characters]
   Confirm Password: [Re-enter your password]
   ```

4. **Accept Terms**
   - Read and accept the Terms of Service
   - Check the privacy policy if desired
   - Enable marketing communications (optional)

5. **Complete Registration**
   - Click **"Create Account"** or **"Sign Up"**
   - Check your email for verification link
   - Click the verification link in your email
   - Your account is now activated!

### Method 2: Invitation from Administrator

If your organization uses multi-tenant setup:

1. **Check Your Email**
   - Look for an invitation email from your administrator
   - The email contains a direct link to join your organization

2. **Click the Invitation Link**
   - Opens the registration page pre-filled with organization details
   - Create your password and complete setup

3. **Verify Your Account**
   - Click the verification link sent to your email
   - You're automatically added to your organization's workspace

## Step 4: Create Your First Client

Before creating invoices, you need at least one client.

### Adding a Client

1. **Navigate to Clients**
   - Click **"Clients"** in the main navigation menu
   - Or click **"Add Client"** from the dashboard

2. **Enter Client Information**
   ```
   Client Name: [Company or individual name]
   Email: [client-email@domain.com]
   Phone: [phone number - optional]
   Address: [Full business address]
   ```

3. **Additional Details**
   - **Tax Number**: GST/VAT/Tax ID if applicable
   - **Default Currency**: Client's preferred currency
   - **Payment Terms**: Net 30, Net 15, etc.
   - **Notes**: Any special instructions or notes

4. **Save Client**
   - Click **"Save Client"**
   - Client is added to your client list

### Client Management Tips

- **Use descriptive names**: Include company name and contact person
- **Complete contact info**: Email is required for sending invoices
- **Set payment terms**: Helps with cash flow planning
- **Add notes**: Track important client information

## Step 5: Create Your First Invoice

Now let's create your first invoice!

### Basic Invoice Creation

1. **Navigate to Invoices**
   - Click **"Invoices"** in the main navigation menu
   - Click **"New Invoice"** or **"+ Add Invoice"**

2. **Select a Client**
   - Click **"Select Client"**
   - Choose from your client list or add a new client
   - Client details auto-populate

3. **Add Invoice Details**
   ```
   Invoice Number: [Auto-generated or custom]
   Issue Date: [Today's date - auto-filled]
   Due Date: [Set based on payment terms]
   Currency: [Auto-selected from client]
   ```

### Add Invoice Items

4. **Add Line Items**
   - Click **"Add Item"**
   ```
   Description: [Service or product description]
   Quantity: [Number of units]
   Unit Price: [Price per unit]
   Tax Rate: [Applicable tax percentage]
   ```

5. **Add Multiple Items**
   - Click **"Add Item"** for each additional line
   - Use **"Duplicate"** to copy similar items

6. **Add Discounts (Optional)**
   - Click **"Add Discount"**
   ```
   Discount Type: Fixed Amount or Percentage
   Amount/Percentage: [Discount value]
   Description: [Reason for discount]
   ```

### Invoice Settings

7. **Configure Additional Settings**
   - **Notes**: Internal notes (not visible to client)
   - **Terms & Conditions**: Payment terms and conditions
   - **Footer**: Additional business information

8. **Review and Save**
   - Click **"Save Draft"** to save without sending
   - Or click **"Save & Preview"** to see the final invoice

### Invoice Preview

9. **Review Your Invoice**
   - Check all details are correct
   - Verify calculations (subtotal, tax, total)
   - Ensure professional appearance

10. **Send Invoice**
    - Click **"Send Email"** to email to client
    - Or click **"Download PDF"** to save locally
    - Invoice status changes to "Sent"

### Invoice Management

- **Draft**: Not yet sent to client
- **Sent**: Delivered to client, awaiting payment
- **Paid**: Payment received
- **Overdue**: Past due date
- **Cancelled**: Invoice cancelled

## Step 6: Record a Payment

When your client pays:

1. **Find the Invoice**
   - Go to **"Invoices"**
   - Click on the paid invoice

2. **Record Payment**
   - Click **"Record Payment"** or **"Mark as Paid"**
   ```
   Payment Date: [Date payment received]
   Amount: [Payment amount]
   Payment Method: [Cash, Check, Bank Transfer, etc.]
   Reference: [Check number, transaction ID, etc.]
   Notes: [Optional payment notes]
   ```

3. **Save Payment**
   - Click **"Save Payment"**
   - Invoice status automatically updates to "Paid"

## Common First-Time Issues

### "I can't access the application"
- Verify the URL is correct
- Check internet connection
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache and cookies

### "Client email is invalid"
- Ensure email format is correct (user@domain.com)
- Check for typos in email address
- Verify client has access to email

### "Invoice calculations are wrong"
- Check quantity and unit price values
- Verify tax rates are correct
- Ensure discount is applied properly
- Review subtotal, tax, and total calculations

### "PDF won't generate"
- Check browser compatibility
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if issue persists

### "Can't upload files"
- Supported formats: PDF, JPG, PNG (max 10MB)
- Check file size limits
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Try a different file or browser

## FAQ

### Account and Access

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to Settings → Security → Change Password. Enter current password and new password twice.

**Q: Can I have multiple organizations?**
A: Yes, with appropriate licensing. Contact your administrator or sales for multi-organization setup.

**Q: How do I reset my password if I'm locked out?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Follow the email instructions to reset.

### Invoices and Billing

**Q: Can I customize invoice templates?**
A: Yes, go to Settings → Invoice Templates to customize colors, logos, and layouts.

**Q: How do I send invoices in bulk?**
A: Select multiple invoices from the invoice list and click "Send Selected" to email them all at once.

**Q: Can I accept online payments?**
A: Yes, integrate with Stripe or other payment processors through Settings → Payments.

### Clients and CRM

**Q: How many clients can I add?**
A: Unlimited clients in all license tiers. Add as many as needed for your business.

**Q: Can I import clients from CSV?**
A: Yes, go to Clients → Import to upload client data from spreadsheet files.

**Q: How do I add client notes?**
A: In client details, click "Add Note" to record interactions, reminders, and important information.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The application is slow. What can I do?**
A: Check your internet connection, try a different browser, or clear browser cache. Contact support if issues persist.

**Q: Can I use the mobile app?**
A: Yes! Download from App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) and log in with your account.

**Q: How do I export my data?**
A: Go to Settings → Data Management → Export Data. Choose format (PDF, Excel, CSV) and download.
